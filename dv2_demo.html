Context

Mid-market fashion retailer: 5 locations, 3 jurisdictions, 6 source systems (1C + Bitrix24 + WMS Excel + site XML + WB/Ozon API). Production-shape DV2.0 modelling lives in Kubernetes-hosted ClickHouse 25.5 + Postgres 17 + MinIO; Argo Workflows orchestrates the refresh DAG; dbt builds the mart layer; per-branch CDC fan-out via two Postgres databases and two CH MaterializedPostgreSQL databases proves operational isolation.
